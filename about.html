About Terravast Holdings

Connecting verified land buyers and sellers nationwide with secure, transparent transactions.

Our Vision

To become Botswana's most trusted digital land marketplace, empowering property ownership and investment for every Motswana.

Our Mission

Provide a secure, structured platform that simplifies land transactions, ensures transparency, and boosts real estate growth nationwide.

Why Choose Us

  • Verified Listings – All properties reviewed before publication
  • Nationwide Coverage – From Gaborone to remote villages
  • Direct Purchase Options – We buy and sell land directly
  • Legal & Ownership Verification – Secure documentation process
  • Dedicated Support Team – Helping you at every step

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Terravast Holdings Pty Ltd emerged from a simple observation: buying and selling land in Botswana was fragmented and lacked trust. We built a digital platform that brings transparency, verification, and nationwide reach to the land market. Today, we connect thousands of buyers and sellers across all districts.
